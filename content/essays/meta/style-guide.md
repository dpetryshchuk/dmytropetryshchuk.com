This is my personal style guide. It documents how I write, what I believe about writing, and the conventions I use on this site.

## Tone

Write plainly. Prefer short sentences. Cut anything that doesn't add meaning.

> The most valuable of all talents is that of never using two words when one will do.
> — Thomas Jefferson

## Quotes

Use blockquotes (`>`) for direct quotes. Always attribute. Format: quote first, attribution on the next line with an em dash.

## Structure

Use `##` for top-level sections. Go no deeper than `###`. If you need more nesting, the section is too complex.

## Status labels

- **draft** — rough, unreviewed
- **in-progress** — actively being developed
- **finished** — complete and unlikely to change
